Spongebob Bathroom

I just had to include this! A while ago, when Spongebob was all the rage, my mom was going to renovate this bathroom. It was getting old and the original theme of it was Mickey Mouse. We asked her if we could pick the new theme and she agreed! We, of course, chose the coolest topic ever: SPONGEBOB SQUAREPANTS!

It always brings a smile to my face when seeing it, and I hope I can have a bathroom as cool as it one day.
