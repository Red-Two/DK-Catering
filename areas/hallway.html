Hallway

The hallway: the first place you walk through when you wake up and the last place you walk through right before you go to sleep.

Our hallway is always a spot of activity, whether a younger sibling is running up and down it or someone is vacuuming it, there is always something that is going on. I remember during my childhood that me and my siblings would play games in it and stand outside each others doors screaming at each other to wake up or ask if they 'borrowed' something of ours. We even had squirt gun battles throughout the hallway, which we got into trouble for so much.

Overall, I'm quite fond of our hallway and it will always mean so much to me.
